About the Hotel

Luxury at the Centre of Sétif

A landmark of modern hospitality in northeastern Algeria, Hôtel Sétif offers spacious rooms with contemporary décor, attentive service, and unbeatable city-centre convenience. Enjoy panoramic views, a celebrated on-site restaurant, and direct tram access.
